## Moving to a Design Sprint Model of Work {#moving-to-a-design-sprint-model-of-work}

![](/ox-hugo/20240407T022200--lens-kanban-zoomed__image.png)
****Sketch.**** Develop concepts and sketches with a focus on look and feel. Create several sketches to choose from.

-   Look through research for use-cases
-   Identify edge cases, create model of business logic
-   Find design patterns in the wild
-   Replicate the current design in Figma
-   Setup calls with experts
-   Sketch ideas of various scope

****Decide**** the right fit for dev cost to implement, impact on other modules, impact on design system.

-   Mix and match elements from different sketches
-   Investigate cost to build
-   Socialize with stakeholders

****Build.**** From sketches to pixel-perfect clickable prototypes.

-   Redesign with current system
-   Improve aesthetics
-   Build testable prototypes

****Test.**** Understand if improved look and feel meet the expectations of users and validate improvements in design.

-   A/B testing between new design and current implementation
-   Validate improvement with stakeholders

****Review.**** Discuss test results and prep for hand-off to development.

****Ship.**** Conversation with development to understand needs
